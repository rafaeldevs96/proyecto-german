Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Steamline Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you visit our website and use our communication services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us through our website forms and chat widgets, including name, email address, and phone number.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing activities over time and across different websites. This may include:

  • IP address, browser type, and pages visited.
  • Data to improve website functionality and analyze trends.
  • Personalized user experiences based on browsing behavior.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. SMS and Text Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) from Steamline Group LLC.

  • Consent Protection: SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time by texting "STOP".

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access.
