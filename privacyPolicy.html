Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Steamline Group LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you visit our website and use our communication services, including SMS and email.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us through our website forms and chat widgets, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number.
  • Professional information for job applications.
  • Vehicle interests and trade-in details for dealership leads.

3. SMS and Text Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number and opting in through our forms or chat widget, you consent to receive text messages (SMS) from Steamline Group LLC.

  • Consent Protection: SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to the number you received the message from. After you send "STOP", we will confirm your unsubscription via SMS.

4. How We Use Information

We use your data to provide customer support, process service requests, manage dealership appointments, and send marketing communications if you have explicitly opted-in.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
